My Dearest Kashvi
Love,

They tell me it is your birthday. A special one, they say. Eighteen.

I have walked this earth for over a thousand years, seen empires rise and fall, and yet, I find myself pausing to acknowledge this moment of yours. Why? Because I recognize power when I see it.

Do not let anyone tell you who you are or who you should be. You are the architect of your own destiny. Paint your life with bold colors, even if you have to use a little red now and then.

The world will try to break you. Do not let it. Be fierce. Be unstoppable. And if you ever need a lesson in how to rule... you know where to find me.

Happy Birthday, simple human. Make it legendary.
- Niklaus Mikaelson
